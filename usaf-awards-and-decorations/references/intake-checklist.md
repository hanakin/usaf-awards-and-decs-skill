# Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before drafting. Missing facts are the main reason award and decoration packages come back weak or unusable.

## Required facts

- Member name, rank, and duty title
- Unit, squadron, group, wing, and installation if relevant
- Award type
- Period of performance
- Suspense or character limits if any
- Local template or example if the unit uses one

## Accomplishment capture

For each accomplishment, gather:

- What the member did
- Why it mattered to mission, readiness, people, or resources
- The measurable result
- The scale
- Who benefited
- Whether the result was sustained, immediate, or both

## Metrics to hunt for

- Dollars saved or recovered
- Hours saved
- Sorties, passengers, patients, or systems supported
- Training completions or qualification rates
- Inspection results
- Readiness percentages
- Workload, case count, or throughput change
- People led, coached, or developed
- Awards, certifications, or evaluations earned because of the work

## If details are missing

Use short placeholders instead of fabricating:

- `[confirm amount saved]`
- `[confirm number of Airmen supported]`
- `[confirm exact award period]`
- `[insert local line limit]`

## Fast intake prompt

Ask the user for:

1. Award type and period
2. Member info and unit
3. Top 3 to 5 accomplishments
4. Metrics for each accomplishment
5. Any local template, line limit, or sample package
